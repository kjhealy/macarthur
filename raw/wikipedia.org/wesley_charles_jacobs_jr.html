Wesley Charles Jacobs Jr.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
  (Redirected from Wesley Charles Jacobs, Jr.)

Wesley Charles Jacobs Jr. is an Oglala Lakota rural planner, who works in South Dakota for the First Nation's Financial Project.[1]

He graduated from University of Massachusetts Amherst with a Master's in Rural Planning, in 1984.[2]

He is on the Intertribal Bison Cooperative Board.[3]

Awards[edit]

  • 1987 MacArthur Fellows Program [4]
