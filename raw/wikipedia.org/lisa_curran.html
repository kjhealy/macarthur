Lisa Curran is an American tropical forester, and Roger and Cynthia Lang Professor in Environment & Anthropology, at Stanford University.[1][2]

Education[edit]

Curran graduated from Harvard University, and Princeton University with a Ph.D.[3]

Career[edit]

Curran was professor of tropical ecology and director of the Tropical Resources Institute at the Yale University.[4]

Curran is a professor at the Santa Fe Institute.[5]

Awards[edit]

  • 2006 MacArthur Fellows Program [6]
