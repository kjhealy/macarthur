David B. Goldstein is an American energy conservation policy expert. He co-directs the Natural Resources Defense Council's Energy Program.[1] He currently serves as the board chair of the Institute for Market Transformation, which he co-founded in 1996.[2]

Life[edit]

He graduated from the University of California, Berkeley with a Ph.D. in Physics.

Awards[edit]

  • 2002 MacArthur Fellows Program
  • Fellow of the American Physical Society
  • Leo Szilard Award for Physics in the Public Interest
  • 2003 California Alumni Association’s Award for Excellence in Achievement
