José A. Quiñonez

José A. Quiñonez
Nationality American
Notable work
 Winning MacArthur Fellows Program

José A. Quiñonez is an American financial-services innovator and winner of the MacArthur Fellows Program. He founded Mission Asset Fund in 2007 in order to legitimize informal peer-to-peer lending networks in order to help marginalized groups build credit and serves as its CEO.[1], [2]

Education[edit]

In 1994, he received a B.A. from the University of California, Davis and an M.P.A. in 1998 from Princeton University.[2]
