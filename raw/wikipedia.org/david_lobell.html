David Lobell is an agricultural ecologist and an Associate Professor in Environmental Earth System Science at Stanford University.[1] He won a MacArthur Award in 2013.

Work[edit]

Lobell's work deals primarily with food security.[2] He also investigates the impact of climate change on crop yields.
