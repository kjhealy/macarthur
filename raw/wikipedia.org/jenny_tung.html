Jenny Tung is an evolutionary anthropology and geneticist scientist. She is an Associate Professor of Biology at Duke University. In 2019, she was awarded a MacArthur Fellowship.[1]

Personal life and education[edit]

Tung is married to fellow Duke professor Sayan Mukherjee.[2]

Tung earned a bachelor's degree in 2003 from Duke University. She remained at Duke for her graduate studies, obtaining a PhD in 2010. After a postdoc at the University of Chicago, she returned to Duke to join the faculty in 2012.[1]'
